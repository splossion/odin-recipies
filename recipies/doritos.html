Doritos Chicken Casserole

Home

Description

I was given this Dorito chicken casserole recipe by a co-worker. My husband says: "You had me at Doritos!"

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 package nacho cheese-flavored tortilla chips (such as Doritos), crushed
  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend, divided
  • 1 Â½ cups salsa
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can corn, drained
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 8 ounces sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Spread crushed chips out on the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. Stir chicken, 1 cup cheese, salsa, corn, condensed soups, and sour cream together in a bowl. Pour chicken mixture over crushed chips into the baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Spread remaining 1 cup cheese over the casserole and bake until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 5 minutes more.
